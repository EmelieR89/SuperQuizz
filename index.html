Super Quizz

In this game you take a guess at a randomly generated number between 1-100. After you have guessed, the game leader instructs you to either go higher or lower. You will get to choose how many bots you want to play against in the next step. One of the players is randomly chosen to start answering. All the current players previous answers will be shown as a list. Who ever guesses the right number first wins. Good luck!
